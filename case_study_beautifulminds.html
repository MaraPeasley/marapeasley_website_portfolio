Just because it's health care doesn't mean it has to be boring.

This website was so fun to design, a mental health care site based on the idea of the brain being like a bee hive, thoughts buzzing around. The client had a tattoo she wanted the color scheme centered around and we ran with the concept.

The one thing we wanted to stay away from was the sterile, all-white websites that so many competitors are using. It needed to be fun and interesting.

Hexagons are the bestagons.
Bees and brains both like 'em.

I started by designing some of the visuals for the site, incorporating the molecule structure of chemicals in the brain like dopamine, as they mirror the hexagonal structure of honeycomb. I also designed simple bee illustrations to add a bit of whimsy without distracting from the content of the pages.

Changing as the business changes. Sometimes you just can't answer everything in your FAQs.

We knew the website would change and shift as the business grew so more pages were designed than initially needed. One of these was a simple FAQ page that later became three separate pages each designed to address more complex needs and questions than the FAQ page could handle alone.
